Adventures in Statue of Liberty National Monument

My Experience

This was one of the best trips i've taken in a while. It was my girlfriend & I who spontaneously decided to take a trip to the Statue of Liberty National Monument. We took a ferry from Battery Park in New York City to Liberty Island. The views of the Manhattan skyline from the ferry were breathtaking. Once we arrived at Liberty Island, we were greeted by the iconic statue.

We spent hours exploring the island, taking pictures, and learning about the history of the statue. We even climbed to the crown of the statue, which offered a stunning view of the harbor. The experience was unforgettable, and I would highly recommend it to anyone visiting New York City.

Explore The Adventure!

Whenever you're bored & looking for something to do in the New York/New Jersey area, look into visiting the Statue of Liberty National Monument. It's a great way to spend a day and learn about the history of one of America's most famous landmarks.
